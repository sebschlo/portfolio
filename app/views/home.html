About

My name is Sebastian Schloesser and I am a student at the University of Pennsylvania. I am currently on my fifth year and pursuing both a BSE in Mechanical Engineering and an MSE degree in the same field with a concentration in Robotics. I am also obtaining a minor in Computer Science. I was born and raised in Guatemala City, and then I lived a year in Germany before making my way to Philly.

This site showcases some of the projects I have been involved with in the last couple of years. Mechanical design and coding are two of my greatest passions, and the field of robotics has allowed me to explore them both equally. I believe that the utility people obtain from technology can be expressed as the derivative of progress and innovation, and it is my goal to contribute to exponential growth in my field.
